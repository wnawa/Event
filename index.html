About the Event

About The Event

We cordially invite you to our First Annual MDX & Fujitsu exclusive Partners and VIP Customers Forum taking place in enchanting Cairo, In heart of history with a charming view of the Nile River. Join us for an unforgettable experience.

Under the motto "Unleash Your Data-Driven Future" you can expect an exceptional program with international keynote speakers, and exchange with top technology expertise and executives on themes such as:

  • How AI is shaping business strategies with Digital transformation.
  • SAP Transformation with Fujitsu.
  • Becoming data-driven while keeping IT resilient
  • Embrace the Data age with Fujitsu & NetApp (Celebrating 25 years of Partnership).
